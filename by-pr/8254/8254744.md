## 描述

- Modernize by using align_up
- Move definition of the trivial CodeHeap::header_size() to header to help inlining, which optimizes align_code_offset and others. No big gain, but we're calling CodeHeap::header_size() 1300+ times on Hello World, and when inlined it's just a constant fold.
