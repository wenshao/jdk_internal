## 描述

Please reviews these manpage updates in relation to "JEP 458: Launch Multi-File Source-Code Programs".  The manpage sources had previously been updated internally at Oracle under JDK-8322478, but the open troff file was not regenerated at the time in mainline (it was for JDK 22).

The main gist of the change is that with multi-file support now available,  occurrences of "single source-file" should just refer to "source-file".
