## 描述

When an `AddI`/`AddL` node's inputs change, its `URShiftI`/`URShiftL` users are not re-queued on the worklist. This causes the `((X << z) + Y) >>> z` optimization in `URShiftINode::Ideal` to be missed, triggering the `VerifyIterativeGVN` assertion.

[JDK-8377389](https://bugs.openjdk.org/browse/JDK-8377389) enabled `URShiftI` verification leading fuzzing to find more issues around it.
