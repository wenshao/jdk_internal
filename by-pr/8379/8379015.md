## 描述

This change converts TraceNewOopMapGeneration and TimeOopMap2 and TraceOopMapRewrites to Unified Logging.  UL doesn't take the tty lock so calling this while stack watermark processing doesn't get a lock inversion bug.  Also, it was left over when we converted the rest.

Tested with tier1-3.
