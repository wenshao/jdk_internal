## 描述

Two `Hashtable`'s in `javax.swing.text.html.CSS.LengthUnit` class are modified only within `<clinit>` block:
1. lengthMapping
2. w3cLengthMapping

We can replace them with immutable maps to avoid Hashtable synchronization overhead.
