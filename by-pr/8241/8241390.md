## 描述

The change fixes a 'deadlock' situation in VM_RedefineClasses::lock_classes() when the current thread is in the middle of redefining the same class. The change is based on the fix [1] suggested in the Jira issue [2] comment.

[1] http://cr.openjdk.java.net/~jiangli/8241390/webrev.00/
[2] https://bugs.openjdk.java.net/browse/JDK-8241390
