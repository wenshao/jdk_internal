## 描述

The copyright year in `test/jdk/java/awt/FontMetrics/ExtremeFontSizeTest.java` was replaced from 2024 to 2025 instead of adding 2025 by [JDK-8357672](https://bugs.openjdk.org/browse/JDK-8357672).

Restore the original year in the copyright header.
