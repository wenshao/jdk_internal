## 描述

Optimizations like `Max(A, Min(A, B)) == A` are possible for ints and longs. This fix enhances the fix in [JDK-8373134](https://bugs.openjdk.org/browse/JDK-8373134) to deal with situation, as hinted by @dean-long [here](https://github.com/openjdk/jdk/pull/28895#discussion_r2632963753).
