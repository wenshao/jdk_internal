## 描述

Hi,

Please review this change to reset GC usage tracking data after a user requested `System.gc()`. 

User-requested Full GCs introduce GC load unrelated to heap size; and in some cases G1 may respond to this GC load by aggressively expanding the heap. So we end up with a larger heap, leading to regression in benchmarks that are very sensitive to heap sizes. 

Prior to JDK-8238686, the heap was shrunk at the end of a System.gc() call, which led to repeated cycles of heap shrinking followed by immediate expansion.

Testing: Tier1
