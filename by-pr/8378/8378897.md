## 描述

Patch adds missing definition of depends_only_on_test_impl in SqrtHFNode to prevent assertion failure during improved handling of node's control input rewiring to dominating test conditions added by[ JDK-8347365. ](https://github.com/openjdk/jdk/pull/29158)

Newly added test no longer results into assertion failure on x86 targets supporting AVX512-FP16 feature.

Please review and share your feedback.
