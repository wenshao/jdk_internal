## 描述

CDesktopPeer.m creates dispatch semaphores via dispatch_semaphore_create in both _lsOpenURI and _lsOpenFile but never releases them. Since the JDK is compiled without ARC, each call to Desktop.open(), browse(), mail(), edit(), or print() leaks around 80 bytes.

The issue is similar to [JDK-8376233](https://bugs.openjdk.org/browse/JDK-8376233)

The fix adds dispatch_release(semaphore) after dispatch_semaphore_wait in both functions, paired with dispatch_retain before the async block to prevent use-after-free on the timeout path.
