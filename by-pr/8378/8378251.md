## 描述

Consider typing `foo.bar.baz` in JShell followed by the `delete previous word` command (Alt + Backspace on Linux). Unlike other REPLs (e.g., Python, Emacs) that truncate the line to `foo.bar`, JShell eagerly deletes the whole text. The similar problem appears also in other "word" related commands (e.g. `Move one word forward/backward`).

The problem is in the JShell's recognition of a "word". JLine (used by JShell) by default considers as a part of a "word" all letters, digits and the following characters `*?_-.[]~=/&;!#$%^(){}<>`.

The proposal here is:
JShell should limit the JLine's "word characters" to letters, digits and `_$` only.
