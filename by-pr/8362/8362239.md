## 描述

Hi, please consider the following changes:

The two methods of the ObjectMonitor class, namely `enter_internal()` and `reenter_internal()`, are reconciled against each other. As a result, `reenter_internal()` is removed, whereas `enter_internal()` is modified.

After [8366659](https://bugs.openjdk.org/browse/JDK-8366659) the reconciliation is straightforward, as the current thread is in the blocked state in both methods. 
 
Tested in tiers 1-7 + extra stress tests.
