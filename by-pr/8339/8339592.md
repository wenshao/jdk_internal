## 描述

A few trivial(?) cleanups to `java.lang.runtime.ObjectMethods`:
- Avoid `MethodType.fromMethodDescriptorString` which needs a classloader
- Use `MethodHandles.zero`
- Remove unused static `MethodHandles` and `MethodTypes`
