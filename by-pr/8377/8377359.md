## 描述

We need to get at least `java.lang.String::utf8` inlined:
```
@ 23   java.lang.String::utf8 (269 bytes)   force inline by CompileCommand
  @ 9   java.lang.StringCoding::countPositives (18 bytes)   force inline by CompileCommand
    @ 7   jdk.internal.util.Preconditions::checkFromIndexSize (25 bytes)   failed to inline: disallowed by CompileCommand
    @ 14   java.lang.StringCoding::countPositives0 (33 bytes)   (intrinsic)
  @ 27   java.util.Arrays::copyOfRange (82 bytes)   inline (hot)
    @ 73   java.lang.Math::min (11 bytes)   (intrinsic)
    @ 76   java.lang.System::arraycopy (0 bytes)   (intrinsic)
  @ 31   java.lang.String::<init> (15 bytes)   force inline by CompileCommand
    @ 1   java.lang.Object::<init> (1 bytes)   inline (hot)
```
Otherwise, the `countPositives` intrinsic will not be used!
```
@ 23   java.lang.String::utf8 (269 bytes)   failed to inline: hot method too big
```

Other platforms with smaller `FreqInlineSize` may need this fix, too.
