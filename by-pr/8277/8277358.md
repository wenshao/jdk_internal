## 描述

Accelerates CRC32-C by utilizing vpclmulqdq similarly to CRC32.  This change achieves ~4x throughput improvement.

5986.947899319073 MB/s => 24041.05203089616 MB/s
5840.02689336947 MB/s => 24898.781468710356 MB/s
