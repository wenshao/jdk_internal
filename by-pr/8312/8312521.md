## 描述

After [JDK-8245241](https://bugs.openjdk.org/browse/JDK-8245241), the field `sun.util.locale.provider.LocaleProviderAdapter#defaultLocaleProviderAdapter` is only written in `<clinit>` and then is not used after.
We can remove it.
