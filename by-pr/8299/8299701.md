## 描述

Please review this change to remove GCCause::_wb_conc_mark and the supporting
implementation.  After JDK-8293824 it is no longer used as the cause when
requesting a GC.

Note: The implementation in ZGC appears to have never been used.  Prior to
JDK-8293824 this cause was only used by a G1-specific WB function.  (In the
deep mists of time it might have also been used to trigger a CMS concurrent
cycle; I didn't bother looking into that.)

Testing:
mach5 tier1
