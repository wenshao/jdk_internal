Last webrev was:

https://cr.openjdk.java.net/~roland/8223051/webrev.03/

This PR includes a few minor changes:

- The change in callnode.cpp that Vladimir requested in: https://mail.openjdk.java.net/pipermail/hotspot-compiler-dev/2020-August/039551.html
