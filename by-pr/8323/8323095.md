## 描述

`e` -> `exception block`
`lphd` -> `loop head`

Also removing an unnecessary space. The successor ids have a space before them.

Examples from `java -Xcomp -XX:+TraceOptoParse -version`:

```
Parsing block #8 at bci [33,39), successors: 9 16(exception block)  loop head
```
