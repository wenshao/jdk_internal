## 描述

Replacing
    `String s = System.getProperty("user.dir");` 
with
    `String s = CDSTestUtils.getOutputDir(); `

Replacing
    `File dir = new File(System.getProperty("user.dir"));`
with
    `File dir = CDSTestUtils.getOutputDirAsFile();`

Passed tier1, hs-tiers 2/3/4 tests.
