## 描述

Add StrictMath microbenchmarks to cover FDLIBM algorithms

The methods that require fdlibm semantics are sin, cos, tan, asin, acos, atan, exp, log, log10, cbrt, atan2, pow, sinh, cosh, tanh, hypot, expm1, and log1p.
