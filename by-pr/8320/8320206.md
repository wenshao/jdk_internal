## 描述

The following intrinsics/stubs are missing vzeroupper:
adler32 (since JDK17)
count_positives (since JDK 9)
chacha20 (since JDK 20)
string indexOfChar (since JDK 9)

Adding the missing vzeroupper to avoid AVX-SSE transition penalties.
