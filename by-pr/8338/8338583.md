## 描述

Malloc overhead because of using NMT equals 18 bytes (size of MallocHeader (16 bytes) + footer canary (2 bytes)). Current approach of total malloc overhead calculation takes into account only header size, but not footer canary, as a result leading to incorrect value of reported tracking overhead.
