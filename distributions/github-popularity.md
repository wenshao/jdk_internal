### Key Observations

- **dragonwell-project/dragonwell8** is the most starred individual JDK downstream repo with 4,319 stars
- **dragonwell-project/dragonwell8** leads in forks with 501, indicating the highest contributor activity
- **Alibaba Dragonwell** has the highest aggregate community engagement across all its version repos
- JDK 8 repos consistently outperform newer version repos in stars and forks (Corretto-8: 2132 stars, Dragonwell8: 4319 stars, Kona-8: 1000 stars)
- Chinese tech companies (Alibaba, Tencent) have strong JDK downstream presence, with Dragonwell8 being the single most popular repo
- GraalVM CE builds repo (1724 stars) ranks highly despite being a release-only repo with no source code
